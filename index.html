Nomino – Privacy Policy

Last updated: 7 December 2025

Nomino (“the App”) is a word-based mobile game designed for entertainment. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

Nomino may collect the following data:

1a. Account Information

If you choose to sign in using Google, Apple, or Email, we may collect:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Profile image (optional)

This data is processed securely through Firebase Authentication.

1b. Gameplay Data

We store gameplay-related data such as:

  • Categories selected
  • Game progress
  • Scores and achievements

This information is used only to enhance your experience and provide core game functionality.

1c. Device Information (Automatically Collected)

We may collect certain non-identifying technical information, including:

  • Device model
  • Operating system version
  • App version
  • Anonymous analytics events

This helps us improve performance, understand usage patterns, and troubleshoot issues.

1d. No Sensitive Data

Nomino does not collect:

  • Financial information
  • Contacts
  • Photos or videos
  • Precise location
  • Health data
  • Biometric data

No such data is accessed or stored by the App.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain core app functionality
  • Save and restore your game progress
  • Improve gameplay, performance, and stability
  • Detect and fix crashes, bugs, and technical issues

We do not sell your personal information or share it with third parties for advertising purposes.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase services, which follow industry-standard security practices and support compliance with frameworks such as:

  • GDPR
  • ISO/IEC 27001
  • SOC 1/2/3

All communication between the App and the backend services is encrypted using HTTPS.

4. Third-Party Services

Nomino uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication – for user sign-in
  • Firebase Firestore / Realtime Database – for storing game data
  • Firebase Analytics – for anonymous usage analytics

You can learn more about Firebase’s privacy practices here:

https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

5. Children’s Privacy

Nomino is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action, including deletion of such data.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights including:

  • Requesting deletion of your account and associated data
  • Requesting access to or a copy of your stored data

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details below. We may need to verify your identity before fulfilling certain requests.
